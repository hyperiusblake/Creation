Creation
A Celebration of Art, Music, and Life

Creation is a festival about celebrating the beauty of life, mixing up “scenes”, and providing a safe space for marginalized individuals to gather and showcase themselves freely.

A portion of the funds generated at Creation events will be donated to a local organization that aligns with our values, which are as follows: the prioritization and uplifting of underserved communities, accessibility to art regardless of financial standing or location, equity for all, and the endorsement of mass social change.

No racism, homophobia, transphobia, body shaming, or hatefulness of any kind will be tolerated. Creation is meant to foster a sense of community in the city between not only artists, but art enthusiasts and appreciators alike.
